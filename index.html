Our Mission

UMC exists to bring micronations closer together, promote peace and cooperation, and help define what a true micronation is.

Our Goals

  • 🤝 Unite Micronations – connect small and large nations for collaboration.
  • 📢 Show What a Micronation Is – help others learn and grow.
  • 🛡️ Protect Each Other – stand up against unfair invasions, like if a big micronation such as Borrowland attacks a smaller one.
  • 🌍 Build a Peaceful Micronational World – with treaties, events, and fair rules.
